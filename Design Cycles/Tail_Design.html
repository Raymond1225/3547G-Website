Tail Design Overview

At the beginning of the season Raymond and I both agreed that we need 2 mechanisms to pick up mobile goals. We already had one, our main lift, and we needed another. We came up with motorized gear with a metal bar attached to it. The idea is we put it underneath the mobile goal and the metal bar goes underneath the lip of the mobile goal. It then proceeds to lift the mobile goal up and we gain possession of it. This is one of the most consistent mechanisms in our robot.
