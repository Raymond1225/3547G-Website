Drive Train Brainstorming

Drive Train Name/Type Pros Cons Conclusion
G-Drive Strong, sturdy, extra build space, and fast No horizontal movement and poor turning Overall a decent drive base with many pros. However, turning is a huge disadvantage.
X-Drive Fast and efficient turning, speed, centered build space, and easier auton creation Not as sturdy Overall we saw this as the best option. While it may not be as sturdy we can reinforce it as we go.
H-Drive Strong, sturdy, fast, horizontal movement, and easier auton creation Uses and extra motor and Due to the middle wheel, the build time would be longer Overall it's a decent drive base but due to the fact that it uses an extra motor we decided against it.
