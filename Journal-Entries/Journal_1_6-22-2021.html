Journal Entry 1

Date: 6/22/2021
Goals:

Review Design Options

CAD

Scavenge Parts

Notebook

Decisions
Questions:

Whether to do a CAD or not.

Should we start scavenging parts?

What is the platform for our notebook?

Solutions:

We decided against a CAD for the time being due to lack of needed resources.

We decided to not scavenge parts yet as we wanted to talk about the notebook extensively tonight and we would not be able to start building even if we did scavenge due to lack of parts.

We decided on a website to host our notebook because it would be impressive, expand our coding skills, and be more organized than a traditional notebook.

Accomplishments/Log of Events:

We started the night with a discussion of our drive base which was earlier decided to be an x-drive. We then did research into the iterations of the design people have built before us in order to gain a better understanding of what we were trying to make and to get our team on the same page about designs. With the talk of designs on our mind CAD was suggested and it was considered thoroughly due to the advantage CAD can have with a lower amount of time spent prototyping. After this our design for picking up bases was discussed and researched. We ended up settling on a similar design to ones that were made in “In the Zone” for its mobile goals due to its ability to quickly move goals. The final event of the night was to discuss the format of our notebook. We ended up with the idea to make a website to host our notebook for the experience of coding in HTML and for the organized format that could be accomplished.
