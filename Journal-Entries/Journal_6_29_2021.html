Journal Entry 4

Date: 6/29/2021
Goals:

Finish drive base frame

Add webpage for how we made the website.

Edit sizing format of pictures to better accommodate mobile users.

Decisions
Problems:

Center bar interferes with motor placement on drive base

Sidebar purpose was unclear

Issues with links to other pages

Solutions:

Move center bar back so it no longer interferes with motor placement

Add “Navigation:” to sidebar for better labeling.

Adjust links so that they work properly.

Accomplishments/Log of Events:

This night was a busy one for the schedule we wanted to keep as the drive base needed to be done by the end of the week and more updates to the website were needed to keep up with the amount of content we wanted to add. For the website tonight I wanted to add a page at the suggestion of my mentor that documented the process of how we learned HTML and how to put up the website in general. During the meeting I asked my mother to look at the website on her phone and it was not legible due to the constriction on the screen. To fix this I adjusted the pictures to auto size themselves based on the screen they were viewed on and I added hyphens at the beginning of each link on the sidebar to make it easier to tell what was part of each link. With this came the addition of the label of navigation to the top of the sidebar for easier understanding. I finished building the frame from the last meeting but then I ran into an issue. I was trying to attach the motors when I realized they were too wide and I could not attach them because the center bar was interfering with them. I removed the center bar and moved it back so that it wouldn't interfere with the motors. The prototype will be done at the next meeting.
