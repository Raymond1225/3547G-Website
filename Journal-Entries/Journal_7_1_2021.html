Date: 7/1/2021
Goals:

Discuss Design for Mobile Goal Lift

Finish drive base

Decisions
Problems:

Measurements for Mobile Goal Lift.

Solutions:

Reviewed the specs for Mobile Goals and built the measurements around them.

Accomplishments/Log of Events:

We started the night by splitting up the work as we usually do. Raymond worked on the mobile goal lift design so that it could be finalized before the build process. After consultation with his partner, who was working on the drive base, they settled on a design that had four attachment points to keep the upper bar straight and one that could extend forward and down to grab the goal before returning to a position that both served the purpose of making it hard to drop the goal and to keep stress off of the motors by having the back bars leaning about 25 to 30 degrees backward. After finishing the frame at the last meeting I needed to finish the rest of the prototype. I attached the motors and bearings, I went and cut down some axles to the right size and attached them. I then put the cortex and battery on. We used the built in drive program to test the drive base and it worked decently. However it has a drift and the wheel wells need to be reinforced.
