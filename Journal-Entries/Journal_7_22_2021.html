Journal Entry 7

Date: 7/22/2021
Goals:

Design lift for picking up mobile goals.

Decisions
Problems:

Mounting 4-bar.

Making a tight fit.

Solutions:

Raise 4-bar ups using c-channel as a spacer and measuring to make sure it fits.

Using screws instead of axles.

Accomplishments/Log of Events:

I started out the night by finding the height that the 4-bar needed to be and cutting four bars of that length. Along with this I found 4 2.5 inch lengths of c-channel to use as mounting points. Those four bars were mounted using 90 degree gussets and spaced properly on another c-channel before being attached to the drive train. When constructing the 4-bar I used screws instead of axles in as many places as possible because they have a tighter fit into bearing flats and gears as well as being held in with lock-nuts instead of shaft collars giving the screws a more permanent hold in the lift. Before the end of the meeting I was able to construct both 4-bars however there was more work to be done before it was a proper lift.
