Journal Entry 12

Date: 8/12/2021
Goals:

Create a locking mechanism for our lift

Decisions
Problems:

Our current locking mechanism for our lift isn't consistent so we need to make a new one.

Solutions:

We made a motorized locking mechanism

Accomplishments/Log of Events:

Our current locking mechanism isn't consistent enough and mobile goals keep falling out. To combat this we made a new locking mechanism which is essentially a 3x14 c-channel attached to a motor so we can lock the mobile goal into our lift. This is much more consistent than our last lock.
