Decisions
Problems:

Wheels need to be reinforced

One of the motors have a different gearbox in it

Solutions:

Used 5 wide c-channel and axles to reinforce the wheel wells

Exchange it for the same gearbox that is in the rest of the drive base motors.

Accomplishments/Log of Events:

As mentioned in the last meeting, our wheel wells were too weak and needed to be reinforced. We decided on reinforcing it with 5 wide c-channels. I cut the 5 wide down to the right size and proceeded to attach them to each wheel well. This worked even better than we thought it would.We also needed to catch up on our journal entries so we wrote them.

As for the code, the one I prepared was unequipped for the task at hand as I had just started learning PROS and the one I wrote was faulty as a result. After trial and error I was able to piece together a code that works by having directional movement on one joystick with turning on the other.
