Journal Entry 10

Date: 8/1/2021
Goals:

Make a locking mechanism for the main mobile goal lift

Make a working auton

Decisions
Problems:

Our main mobile goal lift has an issue where the goals like to slip out of it when we move too suddenly.

Solutions:

I made a locking mechanism

Accomplishments/Log of Events:

Raymond and I got around to drive practice but then we encountered another problem. If our robot moved too fast while holding a mobile goal the goal would fall out. I build 2 different locking mechanisms. The first was three standoffs with rubberlinks on the bottom. The idea was when we picked up a mobile goal the standoffs would go underneath the mobile goal and hold it there. We gave up on this design due to the fact that we could never get it to work quite right, it was always too long or not long enough or even when it did work it wouldn't hold the mobile goal down and it would still fall out. The second design was a different approach. Instead of going from the bottom we went from the top. I attached a standoff and put a rubberlink on the end and this design worked.

Progress was also made on the auton this meeting. I went through the process of figuring out the measurements and math needed to translate realworld inches into encoder ticks (More detailed explanation can be found here).
