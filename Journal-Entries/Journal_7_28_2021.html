Journal Entry 9

Date: 7/26/2021
Goals:

Stop Lift from releasing the goal due to the influence of gravity.

Add a mechanism to hold a second goal.

Add a locking mechanism to the lift.

Decisions
Problems:

We were not able to bring two mobile goals up the platform.

Second goal mechanism was unstable.

Solutions:

Replacing drive train motors to go from 200 rpm to 100 rpm.

Attached metal to the mechanism and the center of the robot.

Accomplishments/Log of Events:

On the code side of the happenings tonight was the tale of how I was able to use some of the training my mentor gave me in c++ to piece together a solution to the problem of gravity. You see we had the common issue of our lift falling down due to gravity and this was not something we could write off because it would invalidate the point of the lift and make it ineffective. I initialized this process by modifying my original code for the movement of the lift to include the essential process of taking the value of the motors position and assigning it to the variable “hold” with this I was then able to tell the robot that if the lift fell five units below the “hold” value it was to set the motors to a rpm of one. The reason I could not set it to a constant one is because when I tried that it would constantly raise the lift at all times and this could get dangerous for the safety of the robot if it kept going. After the mechanism for the second mobile goal was built it also had this problem, thankfully I was able to implement the same fix for that.

Raymond and I had decided on adding a second mechanism to lift another mobile goal. I had the idea of adding an angle bar onto a gear so we could lift it and drag a mobile goal behind us. I built it and attached it to the robot but then I ran into the problem of stabilization. Everytime we would pick up a goal the mechanism would shake and the metal would bend. So I attached some metal to the bottom of the mechanism and attached it to the center of our robot and this fixed the problem. Next we ran into the issue where we could not get up the ramp with 2 mobile goals so we decided to change the gearboxes on the motors of the drivebase to 100 rpm instead of 200 rpm.
