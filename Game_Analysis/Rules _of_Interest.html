Rules of Interest

(SG7) The intent is to avoid locking down the game however it is only illegal on your side of the field so there is the potential to lock down goals on the other side of the field as a strategy. This is something we have to be concerned about because our robot is going to have less power than others and they would get away with this strategy on us due to this weakness. We should ask for full clarification on how this rule is going to be reinforced in competition so we can strategize better around it. Another thing to note is that this rule only applies to more than one goal.
Under the definition of what a balanced platform is a platform can be discounted as balanced if a robot is contacting the field wall transitively via a mobile goal This is something to be wary of.
Clearing the AWP line and scoring a ring on each of our alliance mobile goals rewards us with a win point. This is massively important because it effects our placement on the qualification rankings and is not predicated on our performance in the match. And with each match rewarding 2 points for a win and 1 for a tie that single point is worth half of a match and can catapult us forward in the ranking.
A mobile goal does not have to be completely over the line to count as scored in the alliance zone, it only has to contact the alliance zone.
