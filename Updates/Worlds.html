Worlds Updates

Worlds

Worlds is about a month away and we have a few things in our back pocket to prepare. Our robot has consistently been slow all season and stupidly enough it was due to miscommunication. There are three inserts for Vex motors: 100 rpm, 200 rpm, and 600 rpm. One of us thought that we were running 200 rpm motors and didn’t want to jump to 600 as it would severely hamper our torque. Our second member knew that we were running 100 rpm and forgot about the existence of 200 rpm inserts leaving us in this limbo of thinking that would couldn’t get any faster, we have since cleared up the issue and are switching to 200 rpm motors. With this change our autonomous and skills programs will need to be updated to account for this higher speed as if we go too fast we will not be accurate and the new inserts have half of the encoder ticks per rotation of the old ones (1800 to 900) and since my code counts those ticks I need to half the distance traveled for all of my code. Finally we are going to work with local teams to set up scrimmage matches in order to practice driving with the new speed and two prepare for the upcoming event.
