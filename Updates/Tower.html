Tower Updates

Tower

The challenge I wanted to tackle was, “How do I let our arm extend a good distance in front of us while staying within the 18 by 18?” I decided on creating an arm that could move 180 degrees. After much testing there were a few key factors that influenced my arm’s ability to move in the way I wanted. First, the arms could not be placed above each other as many are. This is because the arms actually need to pass by each other to move in the way I want them to; in a similar vein this also meant that no axels or structural material could be placed above the arm as it would block it. The second big challenge with this design was the attachment points to the claw as they could not by right above each other once again, to overcome this I made a connector that uses standoffs and a section of 5 wide flat plate to create two housings that were of different height and offset from one another.
