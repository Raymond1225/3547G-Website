Competition Strategy Updates

In-Person

Our current strategy for competitions is to gain possession of 2 mobile goals as quickly as we can and then keep possession of them for the rest of the match. During the last 30 seconds we then park our robot along with 2 mobile goals.

LRT

During our last LRT competition we noticed most teams' strategy was to stack 4 mobile goals on the opposite side, scoring them 160 points, and then park with 2 mobile goals on the other ramp, scoring them 110 points. This strategy allows them to possibly win 2 win points by controlling the red and blue zone. Our current strategy combats the other strategy by allowing us to win the match and control the red and blue zones. Say we start in the blue zone, our auton starts and then grabs a neutral goal and brings it back to the blue zone. Once the match starts we pick up the mobile goal on the AWP line with our tail and then drive over to the other side and stack 3 mobile goals on the red ramp, scoring us 120 on the red side. We then proceed to push the last neutral goal into the blue zone and then balance on the ramp with 3 mobile goals, scoring us 180. Not only does this win us the match but it also allows us to control the red and blue zones.
