Tail Updates

Tail

Our tail has gone through many changes since our last notebook entry. Our old tail was just a 1 wide flat plate connected to a gear that would grab the mobile goal from underneath. We also had another prototype of our tail that was essentially a c-channel fashioned into an actual c that would pick up a mobile goal from underneath. While this worked at picking up the mobile goals it was hard to align our robot correctly to pick the mobile goals up. While these designs worked they just were not efficient enough for our robot.

Tail Scrapper

Our new tail is a 5 long c-channel with half-inch standoffs connected at the top. This change has made our tail much more consistent at picking up mobile goals and holding them. We also cut down the c-channel connecting our new tail to the gear so that we are able to lower our tail lower. Another addition was our mobile goal holder. The point of this change was to support and hold a mobile goal picked up by our tail in a safe spot where it can't be moved or grabbed by another robot. These changes have made our robot much more consistent at picking up and holding mobile goals.
