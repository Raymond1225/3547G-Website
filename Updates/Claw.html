Claw Updates

Claw

The claw has had two iterations so far, the first was quickly put together and was hook-like in its design. Our new claw has much more consideration, it is essentially a seesaw with standoffs on one end to catch a goal and a cylinder on the other. Due to the way a mobile goal might slip out of the claw most of the force is being put on the standoffs instead of the cylinder which, along with the large lever that it is on, allows the pneumatic system to easily carry goals.
