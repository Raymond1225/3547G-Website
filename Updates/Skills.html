Driver Skills

Our current drivers skill is to start on the opposite side of where the drivers stand on the side of the AWP line. We start by picking up the goal on the AWP line with our claw and then proceed to drive forward while also pushing the neutral goal into the opposite side of the field. Next we turn our robot around and pick up the mobile goal holding the ramp down with our tail. We proceed to balance 4 mobile goals on the ramp and then we pick up the mobile goal on AWP line and drive over to the side of the field our robot started on and balance our robot, along with 3 mobile goals, on the ramp.
